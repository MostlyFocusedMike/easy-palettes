Welcome to Easy Palettes!

The easy way for designers to experiment with color

Getting Started

To change a color, click on its hex code. A color selector will pop up where you can click and drag around to see different colors. The slider on the side adjusts the brightness. Once you have picked your color, click outside the color selector to hid the pop up.

Copying to Clipboard

"Copy #HEX" copies the code and a hash mark (#FFFFFF) to your computer's clipboard. "Copy HEX" will just copy the code (FFFFFF) to your clipboard. This function may not work for some mobile browsers.

Saving/Loading a Palette

To load a palette into the main boxes, just hit the "Load Palette" button on the preview swatch. To save, simply enter a name for your palette and hit enter or use the "Save Palette" button. Saving a palette with a name that already exists just updates that palette.

Deleting Palettes

To delete a single palette, hit the "Delete Palette" button underneath it in the preview box. To remove all palettes, use the "Clear Palettes" button. But be careful! While the default palettes will always reload if you start the program with 0 palettes saved, your personal creations or edits can't come back once they've been deleted.

Finding Palettes

Scrolling is a pain, so if you remember the palette's name, just use your browsers search function (cmd + f in most cases) and the palette will scroll right to the center for you!

Save Your Work

Easy Palettes will save your work, but only on this device, and only if the history isn't cleared. To save the palettes you really want, copy them individually, or use the "Copy All" button to save all of them to your clipboard at once. Paste them somewhere safe!
